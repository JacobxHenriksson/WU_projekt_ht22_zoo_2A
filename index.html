Welcome to Ohio Zoo!

Ohio Zoo has around 5 billion animals representing all of earth's different species. Here, people get a unique opportunity to experience primarily African and Nordic and hispanic and antarctic animals in nature. Families come here to play, learn, eat at our 5 star rated restaurant (Gordon Ramsey is the cheff) and feel community with the world of animals/bugs/reptiles. Ohio Zoo is also a center for species conservation, biodiversity and research.
